Executive Summary
Creative Stranraer is a community-led creative placemaking initiative delivered by The Stove Network in partnership with local residents, artists, and traders. Over the reporting period, the project engaged 412 participants across 31 events, achieving measurable outcomes across social, cultural, economic, and civic domains. This report presents evidence of impact gathered through a participatory mixed-method evaluation framework.
Social Impact
Survey data collected from 247 respondents indicates that 78% reported feeling more connected to their local community following participation. Loneliness scores (UCLA Loneliness Scale) decreased by an average of 1.4 points. Qualitative testimony confirms the formation of new social relationships across age groups, with particular strength among older adults previously experiencing social isolation.
Economic Impact
SROI analysis indicates a social return of £3.20 for every £1 invested, representing a strong case for continued investment. Trader surveys at monthly market events indicate an average increase of 34% in footfall to local businesses on event days, with several traders reporting new regular customers attributable to project activity.
